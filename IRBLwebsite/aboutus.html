About Us

Our History

Started in 2016 as a project, official league started in 2018. After many years of experience in competitive ro-basketball, owner and comissioner Zazag37 decided to create its own league applying its own rules and its own perspective.

The league is based on LBA hoops, with custom courts for every team which can be changed using a script inside the game, we have stat recorders, video recorders and game analysts for every game, the analysis and videos will be posted on the news section.

Jerseys and customs kits will be provided by the league with a price of 5 R$ normal kits and 7R$ Custom kits. Special events are holded throughout the season which require new jerseys
